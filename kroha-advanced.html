Kroha Advanced

Pair your child’s device on your computer with Kroha Advanced and your child couldn’t delete apps or get around limitations.
Learn more about Kroha Advanced:
USB Connection
You will need your child's device and a USB cable
Security
You will need to know your child's device passcode
Reboot
Your child's iOS device may reboot but no content will be lost!
Version
Child devices need iOS 9.3.6+ or Android 7.0+.
Filter bad web sites
Kroha powerful content blocking features ensure that your kids are protected from over 20 million inappropriate URLs and can see only the search results allowed by Google SafeSearch and Youtube restricted mode. You can also manually add sites to be blocked.
